Who We Are

                    

How it’s going

Regularly listed in the top custom web development companies in Torronto and voted one of the best places to work, VirGenius Solutions offers a full set of services from custom web development to ongoing maintenance & support to clients across all industries in the Torronto, Khulna and throughout the World.

Founded by Shoheb, VirGenius Solutions has been building custom software, crafting complex websites, optimizing SQL databases, and taking clients to the Azure cloud for over 15 years. Our comprehensive technical expertise and lean management approach to projects makes us the perfect partner for your next software project. Let’s make sure it is done right the first time.

Specializing in the Microsoft stack, we are a Gold Certified Microsoft Partner with a talented team of full-stack developers, SQL Server DBA’s, UI/UX designers, Azure architects, Office 365 consultants and project managers.

Our mission as a technology partner is to take over full responsibility for turning your boldest technological ideas into end-to-end solutions.

Regardless of your project’s complexity, we always let you focus on your business objectives and leave all the tech-related issues to us.

                    

With a focus on quality and efficiency, our development services combined with comprehensive UX UI design solutions, ensure that your custom software solution not only meets your functional requirements but also provides an exceptional user experience and gains a competitive edge in your respective industries. Whether it's a web application, mobile app, or enterprise software solution, our in-house team has the expertise and resources to deliver a customized solution that meets your specific needs.
